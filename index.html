Simple Watt/Kg Calc

To use this calculator you will need to know your 5s / 1min / 5min / power numbers and your current FTP. Best place to get these would be from your power curve. Strava has a great one for premium members or services such as Training Peaks or Wattsboard also has them. Wattboard is actually free too.

The figures in this calculator are based on Andy Coggan's power zones. You can see the full list here
